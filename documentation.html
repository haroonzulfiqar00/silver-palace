Standard Required Documents

While requirements vary depending on the specific service and nationality, ensuring you have these baseline documents will drastically speed up your processing time.

Consult Our Experts
  • Passport Copies
    High-resolution scans with at least 6 months of validity.
  • Sponsor ID / CR Copy
    Clear copy of local sponsor ID or Company CR documentation.
  • Passport Size Photographs
    Recent photos with white background (standard size).
  • Educational/Marriage Certificates
    Attested copies depending on visa type (dependent/professional).
